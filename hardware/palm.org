* E2

- 2005
- OS: 5.4.7
- Screen: 320x320
- CPU: 200Mhz Intel [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/XScale#PXA210/PXA25x][XScale PXA255]]
  - instruction set based on ARMv5TE without floating point instructions
- RAM: 32Mb
- IRDA, Bluetooth
- Storage:
  - non-volatile flash
  - SD: Max 1GB with stock driver (see SDHC)
