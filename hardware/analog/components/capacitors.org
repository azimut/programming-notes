- *stores* electricity in ~static electricity~ form
- are _conductors_ separated by an _insulator_

- capacitance = ratio of charge to voltage
  1 couloumn/volt = 1 farad
- Charge (Q) is proportional to Voltage (V)
  Q = C*V
