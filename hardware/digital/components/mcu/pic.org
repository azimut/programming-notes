  - Family
    - 93 - 16C84 (PIC16x84) - EEPROM
    - 98 - PIC16F84 - Flash programmable/erasable
    - 00 - PIC18 - C become predominant
    - 04 - dsPIC - C
    - 07 - PIC32MX - MIPS
    - 13 - PIC32MZ - MIPS
    - 16 - PIC32MM - MIPS - low-power
    - 17 - PIC32MK - MIPS - motor control, IOT...
