* protocols
** what?

A protocol on top of IRDA defines:

  - infrared light freq
  - carrier freq
  - # of devices supported
  - # of commands per device
  - # of bits used in encoding
  - encoding procedure
  - key-to-code mapping
  - data rate, may be variable
  - modulation schemes
