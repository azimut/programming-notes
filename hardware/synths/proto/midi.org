- Musical Instrument Digital Interface
- Also used on: stage lighting, and recording studio equipment
- UART/Serial protocol
  - 31250 bps
- Message of 3 bytes
  1) status
     - 4 bit - note on/of
     - 4 bit - channel
  2) note
  3) velocity
