- *branches* are separated by |
- *branch* is made of *pieces*
- *piece* is an *atom* followed by * + ? or bound
- types
  - extended: egrep
  - basic: ed

* bracket expansion []

  - matches characters on the list
  - ^ negates the match
  - - ranges between chars
  - [..] collating element
